# About the Book

The book **“Physical AI”** was written to explore and explain the evolving relationship between artificial intelligence and the physical world. The author’s motivation behind writing this book is to share knowledge gained through hands-on learning and academic study, and to make complex AI concepts more accessible to learners, students, and technology enthusiasts.

This book represents not only the author’s technical understanding but also his passion for innovation, learning, and contributing to the future of AI-driven systems. As his journey with GIAIC continues, this work stands as a reflection of his growth, curiosity, and dedication to the field of Artificial Intelligence.
