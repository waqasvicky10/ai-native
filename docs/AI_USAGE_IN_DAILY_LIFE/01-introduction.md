# Chapter 1 — Introduction to AI in Daily Life

**TL;DR:** AI is already part of your daily life — in your phone, apps, car, teacher, bank, and home.

## 1. What this chapter covers
- What AI really is (simple explanation)
- Where you already use AI without noticing
- Why learning AI now matters
- How this book is structured

## 2. Core concepts / explanation
Artificial Intelligence ^(AI^) allows machines to learn from data and suggest actions.
Your phone, YouTube, maps and spam filter all use AI.
AI is a tool — not magic and not human.

## 3. Practical examples

